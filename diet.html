Ocean Animal Diets and Feeding Strategies

Some ocean animals graze on seaweed and algae, while others hunt fish, squid, or crustaceans. Filter feeders strain tiny plankton from the water, and scavengers clean up dead material on the seafloor. These feeding strategies help keep ocean ecosystems in balance.
