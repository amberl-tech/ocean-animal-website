Ocean Animal Classification

Ocean animals are classified into several major groups based on their characteristics, biology, and evolutionary history. Each group has adapted uniquely to marine life.

The Four Main Groups of Ocean Animals

Fish

The most diverse group with over 34,000 species.

Marine Mammals

Warm-blooded animals that breathe air and nurse their young.

Sea Reptiles

Cold-blooded reptiles adapted to ocean life.

Invertebrates

Animals without backbones making up 97% of ocean species.

Unique Adaptations

  • Fish: Streamlined bodies, fins, gills
  • Mammals: Blowholes, blubber, flippers
